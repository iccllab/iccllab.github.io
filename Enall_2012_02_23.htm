Motivation

Due to the Internet fast progress in the information technology era, especially in the twenty-fast century, the knowledge acquisition is dimensional and quick to all the people demands around the Internet world. However, there are some guys engage in wrongdoing in the Internet activities (Cyber5space). They did the illegitimate behaviors, for instance, invading the other OS-systems without getting the owners??permissions, planting the malicious programs onto the disallowed directories in file systems and then to curb, infect, and paralyze the system normal operations. Even steal(share) the software/hardware/else resources have been authorized in the registered agencies via Internet channels. In particular, the brute-force penetrating methods is probably made use to retrieve illicitly the plaintext-message. Accordingly, a well-designed crypto-mechanism is anxiously required to safeguard the system security and document storage from all the illegitimate attempts.
Besides, we also pay attention to the studies of fast data searching, well-organized data structure, high-performance data compression, and high-capacity/strong-robustness information hiding (steganograpy/watermarking) on text/image embedding systems, etc.

Moreover, we wish to apply the explored techniques to reinforce police technology in the face of new crime problems in the emerging information era. In other words, the Law enforcement in Cyberspace issue with the security explorations and information well-constructed engineering is the end we are going to.

Now the Information Crypto and Construction Lab(ICCL for short) are located on the second-floor of Information Building, Central Police University, Taoyuan, Taiwan, 333. Our work team is made up of a professor (Dr. SHIUH-JENG WANG) and a number of students (graduate/undergraduate) aiming at developing efficient methodologies to hint the crime activities and seize computer-crime suspects in the Cyberspace address. We would like to compass the ends in hope of the most confidence.
